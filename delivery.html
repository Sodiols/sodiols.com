Delivery Options


Graphic Design Services:

Digital Delivery: For graphic design projects, we primarily deliver the final design files digitally via email or file transfer services. You can expect to receive the initial design concepts within 3-5 business days, and the final design files within 3-7 business days, depending on the complexity of the project.


Web Design Services:

1. Website Mockups: For web design projects, we provide initial website mockups as static visual representations of your future website. The delivery time for website mockups is typically within 5-7 business days, allowing you sufficient time to review and provide feedback on the design.

2. Design Prototypes: Once the website mockups are approved, we develop interactive design prototypes using tools like Adobe XD or Figma. The delivery time for design prototypes is generally within 7-10 business days, ensuring you have an opportunity to experience the website's look and feel before the actual development stage.


Front-end Web Development Services:

1. Code Delivery: For front-end web development projects, we deliver the final codebase that powers your website. The delivery time for the codebase is typically within 7-14 business days, depending on the complexity and scope of the project.

2. Code Repository Access: If you prefer, we can provide access to a code repository such as GitHub or Bitbucket, allowing you to have direct access to the project's codebase. Code repository access will be granted within 1-3 business days after the development phase begins.


Additional Considerations:

The delivery timescales mentioned above are estimates and may vary depending on the specific requirements and scope of each project. We strive to deliver high-quality work within the agreed-upon timeframes.

For larger projects or ongoing collaborations, we may establish project milestones and deliverable timelines to ensure smooth progress and transparent communication.

Throughout the delivery process, we maintain regular communication channels, such as email or project management tools, to address any questions, provide updates, and gather feedback.

If you have specific time constraints or deadlines, please let us know during the project planning phase, and we will make every effort to accommodate your needs.


If you have any specific delivery preferences or questions regarding our services, please don't hesitate to reach out to our team. We're here to ensure a seamless and satisfactory experience for all our clients.

Remember to adapt and customize the delivery options and timeframes to align with your specific services and company practices.
